January Flower-Carnation
January is the first moth of the year so the birth flower of january hold the meaning of devotion,loyality, and love.

February Flower-Primrose
For the month of february the birth flower is pretty primrose which symbolizes faithfulness, humility and spiritual wisdom.

March FLower-daffodils
For the march born kid ths birth flower is daffodils. this flowers symbolize rebirth, luck, vanity and prosperity.

April Flower-Sweet Pea
The birth flower for april are the sweet pea and daisy, symbolizing innocence, loyality and transformation.

May Flower-Hawthorn
For May, the birth flower are hawthorn. these flowers have a meaning of humility, sweetness, the return of happiness and motherhood.

June babies are lucky enough to have the honeysuckle as their birth flowers. This june birth flower mean beauty, love, and honour and devotion.

The july birth flower is delphinium which means open heart, positivity, dignity and grace.
